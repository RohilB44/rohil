As previously mentioned manga and anime have generes too, if they didn't have generes it would be hard to know what you are reading or watching. Now let me list a few bellow.

  • Action/Adventure

    • Action/Adventure is basically what the name states. The protagonist has to go around search for an objective. The protagonists are usally nomadic and they have a goal.

  • Shonen

    • Shoenen is a term not many people know, the Jappanese meaning means young boy. Like the meaning the shows are generally made for young boys.

  • Shoujo

    • Just like Shonen it means young girl. These shows are usally meant for girls, and mosly have romance and love. The storeies usally have a different style of drawing.

  • Iseakai

    • Isekai is a term used for anime that take place in multiple worlds. Sekai means world in Jappanese, so that is a big clue to it's meaning. Iseaki generally are action/adventure

  • Thriller

    • Thriller anime generally are intense. Just like normal thriller you don't know whats goning to happen. Thriller anime are generally darker than usal anime.
