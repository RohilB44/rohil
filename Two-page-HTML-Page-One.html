Video Game Generes

Video games have many generes, like I have mentioned before. Generes define a video game and how it functions, without generes it would be hard to know what type of video game you are playing.For example purposes, let me list a few generes and what they are about.

  • Gacha games

    • Gacha games are grindy games that invole animated characters, Gacha games can vary from game to game, but the games are usally hard, and tedious to play.

  • FPS (first person shooter)

    • FPS or first person shooter are games where you can choose a character, or you have to fly out and find weapons, bascally you have to kill the other players for points.

  • Action games

    • Action games are often games with brutal figting, and some even have violence, while some games are not for younger audiences it's still fun to play.

  • Adventure games

    • Adventure games are games with a big world, and you have to go from area to area, but the places you can go are very limited, and narrow.

  • Open world games

    • Open world games with an even bigger world where you can go from area to area, but you can go at your own pace, and go wherever you want.

  • RPG games

    • RPG's are long games, which have an adventure style, but your items and materials can be anything that increases performance, these games are generaly complex.

  • Sport games

    • Sport games are games which invole a real life sport, and you can use real life players in the game you can play Basketball, Soccer, or Football depending on the game.

  • Strategy games

    • Strategy games are games that can even include board games like chess which require thinking and proper execution. Strategy games are often 1v1's with people.

  • Racing games

    • Racing games are where you control a car, and where you can build a car, and you have to be faster than the opponet, hence the term Racing.

  • Comptitive games

    • Competitive video games can usally range up to 1-10 players, and in the games you can fight with each other using different charcters or, you can even 1v1, and compete.
